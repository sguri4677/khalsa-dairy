Frequently Asked Questions

What are your working hours?

Our farm operates from 6 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Saturday. Customer support is available from 8 AM to 8 PM.

Do you deliver nationally?

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery through our trusted logistics partners.

Are your products organic?

Absolutely. All our dairy products are made from organic, ethically-raised cattle.

How can I place an order?

You can order directly through our website or contact us via phone or email. We also have a WhatsApp chat for quick inquiries.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No, we cater to both bulk and individual orders.

Can I visit the farm?

Yes, farm visits are welcome by prior appointment. Contact us to schedule your visit.

How do I become a distributor?

Please contact our sales team for distributor inquiries. We are expanding our reach and welcome partnerships.
